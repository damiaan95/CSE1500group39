How to play:

Drag the piece you want to move to the square you want to move it to.
If this is an allowed move, the piece will have been moved to its new location.
Another option is to click on the piece you wanna move and then click on the square you want to move it to.

Image of the action performed.

The circles on the board when you click or hold a piece indicate the locations the pieces can be moved to.
